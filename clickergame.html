Clicker Game

This project is a simple clicker game that simulates the experience of clicking to earn points. It is designed to be a fun and interactive game, easy to make and play!

How to play:
1. Click the button to earn points.
2. The points will increase with each click.
3. You can buy upgrades to increase your points per click.
4. You can also buy auto-clickers to earn points automatically over time.
5. You can rebirth, which will reset your points but give you a multiplier for future clicks.
6. You can spend a large amount of points to rename the button!
7. You can increase the autoclicker speed to earn points faster.
8. Gaining over a certain number of points will upgrade you to the next tier.

With that being said, just play and have fun!
